Forumla One Podium Predictor

Being interested in both Formula One and Machine Learning, I created a binary classifier to be able to predict whether a specific driver would get a podium at the following race using decades of previous race and weather data.

With my classifier, I was able to correctly predict if a driver would be celebrating 80% of the time!

Chipless Poker

Last summer, I travelled to Qatar for a study abroad. My cohort wanted to play poker (no money of course!) but there wasn't a store in the country that sold chips!

In just a few hours, I was able to develop a program that kept track of all of our bets and winnings so that we could pass the time.
